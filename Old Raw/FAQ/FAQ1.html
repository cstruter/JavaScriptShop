How to enable Cookies
 

Definition

Cookies are small text files that Web sites place in your computer to help your browsers remember specific information. For example, they might store your passwords and user IDs.

 

Internet Explorer 6.0
Select Internet Options… from the Tools menu option
Select the Privacy tab
Click on the Edit button under Web Sites
Type in http://www.shilo.co.za in the box and click on Allow
Then click on OK

 

Internet Explorer 5.0
Select Internet Options… from the Tools menu option
Select the Security tab
Click on the Custom level button
Scroll down to the cookies section
Enable both the "Allow cookies that are stored on your computer" and "Allow per-session cookies (not stored)"

 

Internet Explorer 4.0
Select Internet Options… from the View menu option
Select the Advanced tab
Scroll down to the cookies section
Enable "Always accept cookies"

 

Netscape 4.0
Select Edit from the menu bar
Select Preferences from the Edit menu option
Click on the Advanced in the Category tree structure
Choose accept all cookies

 

Netscape 7

Select Edit from the menu bar
Select Preferences from the Edit menu option
Click on the Privacy & Security
Click on cookies
Click on enable all or enable cookies for the originating website
