How to use this shopping system
 
You need Javascript and cookies enabled to be able to use this system
 
Currently on your system:

Step one:
Click on the product tab.
 
Step two:
Select products you wish to purchase, by clicking on
the add/remove button or by typing the quantity into
the textbox next to the item.
 
Step three:
If you wish to remove any products from the cart, 
simply click on the view cart tab and alter your cart
from there.
 
Step four:
Click on the Check out tab and complete the fill in
form.

Payment
